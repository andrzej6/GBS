Introducing our New Official Supporter
Little Nudge
  • Get Britain Standing® is delighted to announce Little Nudge as its new Official Supporter
  • Little Nudge is a non-intrusive, on-screen reminder (notification App) to perform easy, but, healthy activities. 
  • Clinically designed by physiotherapists and successfully trialled with BUPA,  Little Nudge is a proven method of interrupting prolonged sitting of office workers and prevents and reduces pain as a result of computer work.
  • Little Nudge can be customised to adjust “where” on the screen the reminder appears, “how often” it pops-up and “how long” it stays.
