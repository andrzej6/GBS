Expert Statement
  • Last week the British Journal for Sports Medicine published an Expert Statement which was jointly commissioned by Active Working C.I.C (owners of the Get Britain Standing campaign)  jointly with Public Health England (PHE) entitled: “The Sedentary Office:  a case for change”.
  • This is the first time globally that expert recommendations have been provided for standing in the office workplace.
  • This statement was authored by Prof. John Buckley with input from leading international experts on sedentary behaviour. It has now been distributed to 8,000 members of the scientific community and received global media attention in BBC World TV.
  • The Expert Statement can be downloaded here.
