Active Working Summit™ – 2016
Early bird discount ends 15th October 
  • The Active Working Summit™ 2016 is aimed at senior professional working in  HR, Facilities, Workspace and Workplace Health.

    • Keynote speeches from Prof. James Levine (Mayo Clinic) and Baroness Tanni Grey-Thompson (Chair of ukactive)
    • Leading developments in health and workplace evidence around sedentary behaviour and corporate physical activity interventions
    • Key stakeholder organisations views on the implications concerning the workplace
    • Presentation and discussion on active working solutions and their cost / benefit to employers

Workplace Research
Companies in Cambridge Required
  • We have been approached by University of Cambridge who are we interested in conducting research on the use of Sit-Stand desks in UK workplaces.
  • The costs of sit-stand equipment may be covered by the research budget.
  • If you know a company or organisation nearby Cambridge and interested in taking part in a controlled trial or are planning on installing Sit-Stand desks in the immediate future and are interested in formal evaluation we would like to hear from you.
  Please contact info@activeworking.com
