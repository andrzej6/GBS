Welcome to our second quarterly newsletter. It continues to be a very hectic and  trailblazing year for us. We are delighted that the Get Britain Standing campaign now features regularly in the national press and across radio. Furthermore we have made appearances on made BBC TV and BBC World News. 
So here are some or our headlines:

On Your Feet Britain
  • A huge thank you for all of you who participated in On Your Feet Britain on 24th April.
  • Nearly 500 companies and 100,000 employees participated in the inaugural 2015 #OYFB day, which also trended #3 on Twitter.
  • Get Britain Standing campaign annual workplace awareness run in partnership with the British Heart Foundation raised vital funds for the fight against heart disease.

Expert Statement
  • Active Working C.I.C commissioned an Expert Statement jointly with Public Health England (PHE) entitled: “The Sedentary Office:  a case for change”.
  • This statement was authored by Prof. John Buckley with input from leading international experts on sedentary behaviour. It is due for publication in the very near future.
