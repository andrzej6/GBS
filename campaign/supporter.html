Would you like to become an Official Supporter of the Get Britain Standing campaign?
We have had amazing support from Osteopath, Chiropractors & Physiotherapists since we launched the Get Britain Standing campaign last year and  On Your Feet Britain workplace event last week. 

To thank you, we have decided to set up a FREE* “Official Supporter” package which includes the following:
  1. Official Supporter Osteopath, Chiropractors & Physiotherapist listing on the Get Britain Standing website (averaging over 1,000 unique visitors daily). These listings will be split by category and your listing will be according to date of sign-up.
  2. You will be entitled to insert the Get Britain Standing  logo / web link on your website as an Official Supporter (subject to guidelines and conditions).
  3. We plan to provide your practice with Get Britain Standing  posters later in 2015.
  4. You will be provided with discount vouchers for sit-stand equipment to give to your patients. 
If you are interested in becoming an “Official Supporter” please register here today and INPUT a request in the message box.

Together we will help Britain stand-up, sit less, and move more.

Thanks again from all the team at Get Britain Standing!

*Terms and conditions apply. This offer is only available to Osteopaths, Chiropractors & Physiotherapists who are members of a recognised official body and do not sell office equipment or sit-stand solutions. Vouchers are provided by sit-stand.com however Active Working C.I.C (Community Interest Company) reserve the right to end this offer at any time. Your data may be shared with any future provider of voucher offers.
