On Your Feet Britain - 5 days to go
1 Million Office Workers Now Signed Up

The national media are beginning to get very excited about On Your Feet Britain this Friday.  Last year we trended no.3 on Twitter, with both BBC Breakfast, ITV Good Morning and Radio 4 PM all getting involved by presenting their shows standing. What will you do?

With just 5 days to go we would like to suggest some simple ideas to help make your On Your Feet Britain event as successful as possible:
  1. Send an email around to everyone making sure your co-workers are fully aware of what #OYF2016 is all about. Sample emails are available at the bottom of the OYF download page.
  2. Complete all your downloads early. We will be experiencing huge website traffic this week. To avoid the last minute rush please try to complete all your downloads by WED 27th APRIL to avoid disappointment.  
  3. Pass it on! Please invite your friends, family or contacts in other companies to take part. It is never too late to register
You will be 1 of over 2,000 organisations and 1 million office workers who will be uniting together to participate in a variety of fun and simple activities to #SitLess and #MoveMore at work.
 
We look forward to getting everyone wiggling and waggling together!  Remember the #OYF2016, and try to get people posting on all our social media.
